HOW IT WORKS

From First Ride
to Full Fleet Control

01

Rider Signs Up

Customer scans QR code, fills in personal info, verifies phone number, sets a password and downloads the Ionex App.

02

Dealer Prepares Vehicle

Staff uses the Utility App to scan QR codes, pair batteries via NFC, and deliver the vehicle to the customer on pick-up day.

03

Operator Monitors Network

Operators use iEnergy Console to track assets, review events, manage billing and provide real-time customer support.

04

Fleet Manager Scales

Fleet managers activate plans, track live vehicle locations and manage entire enterprise operations from iFleet Console.
